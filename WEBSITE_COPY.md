## üìã FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (Complete List)

### General Questions

**Q1: What is FreightLabs?**

A: FreightLabs is an AI-driven freight matching and optimization platform that connects Shippers, 3PLs, and Carriers to move freight faster, more transparently, and more profitably. Our platform focuses on capacity utilization, smart matching, fraud prevention, and real-time visibility.

**Q2: How is FreightLabs different from traditional load boards?**

A: FreightLabs is not just a load board. We are a freight optimization ecosystem featuring AI-assisted matching (RouteForge, LaneMaker), capacity-based matching (space, weight, route), built-in fraud prevention and verification, transparent pricing and visibility, and designed to reduce empty miles and deadhead.

**Q3: Who can use FreightLabs?**

A: FreightLabs is built for three key user types: Shippers moving freight, 3PLs and Freight Brokers, and Carriers including Owner-Operators and Fleets.

---

### Shipper Questions

#### Onboarding & Account Setup

**Q4: How do shippers onboard with FreightLabs?**

A: Shippers can onboard through company registration (EIN / Tax ID), business verification, authorized user setup, and payment method setup (ACH / credit options where applicable). Onboarding is designed to be fast, secure, and compliance-ready.

**Q5: What information is required to post a shipment?**

A: Minimum required: Pickup & delivery locations, Pickup & delivery date/time, Weight, Pallet count or dimensions, Equipment type, and Offered rate or pricing preference. Optional (but recommended): Commodity, Temperature requirements, and Accessorials.

#### Freight Matching & Execution

**Q6: How does FreightLabs match my shipment to carriers?**

A: FreightLabs uses AI-driven matching based on carrier location and availability, equipment compatibility, available space and weight, historical performance, and route efficiency. This reduces delays, rejections, and empty miles.

**Q7: Can I choose or approve the carrier?**

A: Yes. Depending on your settings, you can auto-assign the best match, approve suggested carriers, or work with preferred carrier lists.

#### Visibility & Transparency

**Q8: Do I get shipment visibility?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs provides status updates (Booked, Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered), timestamped milestones, and exception alerts for delays or issues.

**Q9: Can I see pricing breakdowns?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs promotes transparent pricing, including linehaul, accessorials (if applicable), and agreed rates upfront.

#### Security & Fraud Prevention

**Q10: How does FreightLabs protect against fraud?**

A: FreightLabs uses EIN and business verification, carrier authority checks, identity validation, behavior-based fraud detection, and restricted load access controls.

**Q11: Is my data secure?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs uses encrypted data storage, role-based access, secure payment handling, and industry-standard security practices.

---

### 3PL / Broker Questions

#### Onboarding & Compliance

**Q12: How do 3PLs onboard?**

A: 3PL onboarding includes business verification, broker authority validation, user role setup, and compliance review. This ensures trusted counterparties on the platform.

**Q13: Can multiple agents use one account?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs supports multi-user accounts, role-based permissions, and agent-level visibility and controls.

#### Freight Matching & Capacity Optimization

**Q14: How does FreightLabs help 3PLs move freight faster?**

A: FreightLabs matches loads to available capacity (not just trucks), reduces manual calls and emails, suggests best-fit carriers instantly, and helps cover partials and tight lanes.

**Q15: Can I post partial or flexible loads?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs is built to match partials, optimize unused space, and reduce deadhead and underutilization.

#### Transparency & Control

**Q16: Can I track loads in real time?**

A: Yes. You get status updates, carrier confirmations, delivery timestamps, and audit-ready records.

**Q17: Can I set pricing rules?**

A: Yes. 3PLs can set floor/ceiling rates, control margin preferences, and use dynamic pricing tools (future roadmap).

#### Risk & Fraud Protection

**Q18: How does FreightLabs protect brokers from double brokering?**

A: FreightLabs actively monitors carrier authority mismatches, unusual booking patterns, identity inconsistencies, and suspicious rate behavior.

---

### Carrier Questions

#### Onboarding & Eligibility

**Q19: Who can onboard as a carrier?**

A: Owner-operators, small fleets, large fleets, and drivers of box trucks, straight trucks, and sprinters are all supported.

**Q20: What documents are required for carrier onboarding?**

A: Typically: MC / DOT authority, Insurance (COI), W-9, and Equipment details.

**Q21: How long does carrier onboarding take?**

A: Most carriers can onboard in minutes, assuming documents are ready.

#### Load Matching & Revenue

**Q22: How does FreightLabs help carriers make more money?**

A: FreightLabs focuses on reducing empty miles, matching partial capacity, optimizing routes, and offering consistent freight through tools like LaneMaker.

**Q23: Do I need a dispatcher?**

A: No. FreightLabs is designed so owner-operators can self-manage, fleets can reduce dispatcher workload, and automated matching handles routine decisions.

#### Transparency & Trust

**Q24: Are rates guaranteed for carriers?**

A: Yes. Rates are clearly displayed before acceptance, locked once confirmed, and visible without hidden fees.

**Q25: Can I see pickup and delivery details upfront?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs promotes full transparency with exact locations, time windows, and load requirements.

#### Payments & Protection

**Q26: How do carriers get paid?**

A: Payment options may include standard payment terms, factoring integrations (roadmap), and clear invoice records.

**Q27: How does FreightLabs protect carriers from scams?**

A: We protect carriers through verified shippers and brokers, load authenticity checks, restricted access to sensitive loads, and monitoring suspicious activity.

---

### Platform Questions

**Q28: Does FreightLabs replace my TMS?**

A: FreightLabs can integrate with existing workflows, act as a lightweight operational layer, or serve as a standalone solution for smaller operators.

**Q29: Is FreightLabs mobile-friendly?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs is designed for dispatchers, drivers, and operators on the go.

**Q30: Is FreightLabs still evolving?**

A: Yes. FreightLabs is built as a modular ecosystem, with ongoing releases including advanced analytics, deeper factoring integrations, predictive pricing, and expanded visibility tools.

---

### Support Questions
